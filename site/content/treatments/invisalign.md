### Recovery Tips

- Wear aligners 20-22 hours per day. Only remove them for eating, drinking (anything other than water), and brushing.
- Clean aligners with lukewarm water and a soft toothbrush. Avoid hot water which can warp the plastic.
- Brush and floss after every meal before reinserting aligners to prevent trapping food and bacteria.
- Store aligners in their case when not in use — never wrap them in a napkin (they get thrown away).
- Keep your previous set of aligners as a backup in case a current set is lost or damaged.
- Mild discomfort with each new aligner is normal. Switch to new aligners at bedtime to sleep through the initial tightness.
