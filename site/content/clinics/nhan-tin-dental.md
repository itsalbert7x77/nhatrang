## Services Breakdown

### Dental Implants
Implant placement is Nhan Tin Dental's primary specialty. The clinic has placed implants for over two decades, building extensive case experience across different clinical situations. Both standard and complex implant cases are handled, including immediate placement after extraction and delayed placement following bone grafting.

The clinic works with established Korean implant systems that offer reliable osseointegration at budget-friendly pricing. The full implant process (post, abutment, and crown) is managed in-house.

### Bone Graft Surgery
This is where Nhan Tin truly differentiates itself. Many patients are told by other clinics that they cannot receive implants due to insufficient jawbone. Nhan Tin's **bone graft surgery expertise** opens the door for these patients through procedures including:

- **Socket preservation grafts** after tooth extraction
- **Ridge augmentation** to widen or heighten the jawbone
- **Sinus lift surgery** to create space for upper jaw implants
- **Block bone grafts** for severe bone deficiency

The clinic's 20+ years of surgical experience in this area provides confidence for patients facing these more complex procedures.

### Braces (Orthodontics)
Nhan Tin offers traditional metal braces for teeth straightening. This is a practical option for patients who plan to stay in Vietnam for an extended period or who want to start treatment at an affordable price point.

### Porcelain Crowns
The clinic provides porcelain crown restorations including metal-ceramic and zirconia options. Crowns are commonly placed as the final step in implant restoration or to restore damaged natural teeth.

### Inlay/Onlay Restorations
For teeth with moderate damage that do not require full crowns, Nhan Tin offers custom-fabricated inlay and onlay restorations. These preserve more natural tooth structure than crowns while providing superior durability compared to standard fillings.

### Teeth Whitening
Professional in-office whitening services are available at budget-friendly rates, providing an easy cosmetic upgrade during a dental tourism visit.
