## What Is a Dental Implant?

**A dental implant is a titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone to permanently replace a missing tooth. In Nha Trang, dental implants cost $500-$1,400 — saving you up to 85% compared to Australia, where the same procedure runs $3,000-$6,500. Top-rated clinics like Viet Han Dental (4.8 stars, 119 reviews), Opal Dental (4.8 stars, 234 reviews), and Dental Clinic Dr. Nguyen (4.8 stars, 151 reviews) use internationally recognised implant systems from Korea, Switzerland, and Germany.**

Dental implants are considered the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. Unlike dentures or bridges, implants fuse with your jawbone through a process called osseointegration, providing a stable, permanent foundation for a custom-made crown. The result looks, feels, and functions exactly like a natural tooth.

### How the Procedure Works

The dental implant process in Nha Trang typically involves these steps:

1. **Consultation and imaging** — Your dentist takes a panoramic X-ray or CBCT scan to evaluate bone density and plan implant placement.
2. **Implant placement** — Under local anaesthesia, the titanium post is surgically inserted into the jawbone. This takes 30-60 minutes per implant.
3. **Healing period** — Over 3-6 months, the implant fuses with the bone (osseointegration). A temporary crown may be placed during this time.
4. **Abutment and crown** — Once healed, an abutment connector and custom porcelain crown are attached, completing the restoration.

Some clinics offer **immediate-load implants** where a temporary crown is placed on the same day as surgery, ideal for dental tourists with limited time.

### Recovery Tips

- Stick to soft foods (soups, yoghurt, smoothies) for the first week after surgery.
- Avoid smoking for at least 2 weeks — it significantly impairs healing.
- Apply ice packs to the cheek to reduce swelling during the first 48 hours.
- Take prescribed antibiotics and pain medication as directed.
- Avoid strenuous physical activity for 3-5 days after surgery.
- Rinse gently with warm salt water starting 24 hours after the procedure.
