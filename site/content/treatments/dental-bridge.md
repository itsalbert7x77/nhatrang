## What Is a Dental Bridge?

**A dental bridge is a fixed prosthetic that replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring to the teeth on either side of the gap. In Nha Trang, dental bridges cost $180-$500 for a 3-unit bridge — saving up to 85% compared to Australia ($2,000-$5,000) and the USA ($2,000-$5,000). Viet Han Dental (4.8 stars, 119 reviews), Opal Dental (4.8 stars, 234 reviews), and Dental Clinic Dr. Nguyen (4.8 stars, 151 reviews) offer high-quality bridge work in porcelain, zirconia, and emax materials.**

Bridges are an excellent option for patients who want a fixed (non-removable) tooth replacement without the surgery required for dental implants. They restore your ability to chew, speak, and smile with confidence, and they prevent adjacent teeth from shifting into the gap over time.

### Types of Dental Bridges

- **Porcelain-fused-to-metal (PFM)** ($180-$280 for 3 units) — Metal substructure with porcelain overlay. Durable and affordable.
- **Full zirconia** ($300-$400 for 3 units) — Extremely strong and aesthetic. No metal, no risk of dark margins showing.
- **Emax** ($350-$500 for 3 units) — The most natural-looking option with exceptional light transmission.
- **Implant-supported bridge** ($1,500-$3,500) — Anchored to dental implants instead of natural teeth. Ideal for larger gaps or when abutment teeth are not suitable.

### Recovery Tips

- Mild sensitivity around the abutment teeth is normal for the first 1-2 weeks.
- Avoid chewing hard or sticky foods on the new bridge for the first few days.
- Clean under the pontic (false tooth) daily using a floss threader, interdental brush, or water flosser.
- Return to the clinic if the bite feels uneven — a simple adjustment resolves this.
- Maintain regular dental check-ups to monitor the bridge and supporting teeth.
