## What Is a Dental Emergency?

**A dental emergency is any urgent dental situation requiring immediate professional attention — including severe toothache, broken teeth, knocked-out teeth, abscesses, and lost restorations. In Nha Trang, emergency dental care is accessible, affordable, and high-quality. ### Common Dental Emergencies

- **Severe toothache** — Often caused by deep decay reaching the nerve. Requires examination, X-ray, and either root canal or extraction.
- **Broken or chipped tooth** — Can be repaired with bonding, a temporary crown, or veneer depending on severity.
- **Knocked-out tooth** — Time-critical emergency. Reimplantation is most successful within 30-60 minutes.
- **Lost filling or crown** — Quickly replaced with a temporary or permanent restoration.
- **Dental abscess** — Infection causing swelling and severe pain. Requires drainage, antibiotics, and follow-up treatment.
- **Broken denture or orthodontic wire** — Repaired or adjusted to restore function and comfort.

### What to Do in a Dental Emergency

1. **Stay calm** and assess the situation.
2. **For a knocked-out tooth**: Handle by the crown, rinse gently, keep moist in milk, and get to a clinic immediately.
3. **For severe pain**: Take over-the-counter ibuprofen (not aspirin, which can increase bleeding). Apply a cold compress.
4. **For a broken tooth**: Rinse your mouth with warm water. Save any broken pieces.
5. **For a lost crown**: Keep the crown safe. Apply dental cement or denture adhesive temporarily.
6. **For an abscess**: Rinse with warm salt water. Do not try to drain it yourself. Seek professional care urgently.
7. **Contact a clinic**: Call or visit one of the recommended clinics above. Most accept walk-ins for emergencies.

### Recovery Tips

- Follow all prescribed medication instructions, especially antibiotics for infections.
- Stick to soft foods if you had an extraction or root canal.
- Apply ice packs for swelling — 20 minutes on, 20 minutes off.
- Avoid hot beverages and alcohol for 24 hours after emergency treatment.
- Schedule a follow-up appointment to ensure proper healing and complete any permanent restoration.
