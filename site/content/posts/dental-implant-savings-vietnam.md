## Why Are Implants So Much Cheaper?

The price difference is not about quality. It is about economics:

**Lower operating costs.** Rent for a dental clinic in Nha Trang is a fraction of what it costs in Sydney, London, or Los Angeles. Utilities, insurance, and administrative costs are all proportionally lower.

**Lower labor costs.** Vietnamese dentists are highly trained, but salaries reflect the local cost of living. A skilled implantologist in Vietnam earns well by local standards while charging patients far less than a Western counterpart.

**Lower lab fees.** Dental laboratories in Vietnam produce high-quality crowns and prosthetics at much lower cost than Western labs.

**Same materials.** The implant itself -- an Osstem, Straumann, or Nobel Biocare fixture -- is a globally manufactured product. The clinic pays a similar wholesale price regardless of location. The savings come from everything surrounding that implant: the surgery, the lab work, the overhead.
