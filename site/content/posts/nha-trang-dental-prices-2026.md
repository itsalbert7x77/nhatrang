## Price Differences Between Clinics

Prices vary across Nha Trang's clinics for several reasons. Clinics like Daisy International Dental and Viet Han Dental position themselves slightly higher due to imported equipment and concierge services. Nhan Tin Dental tends to offer the most budget-friendly rates. Opal Dental and Dr. Nguyen sit in the mid-range, offering an excellent balance of quality and value.

## Hidden Costs to Watch For

Always ask for an itemized, all-inclusive quote before committing. The main extras to watch for include:

- **Temporary crowns** during the waiting period between visits ($10-$20 each)
- **Medications** prescribed after surgery ($5-$15)
- **Additional X-rays or scans** if complications arise ($10-$40)
- **Credit card processing fees** of 2-3% at some clinics
- **Lab fees** for complex prosthetics (should be included in your quote)

## How to Get the Best Price

Send your dental records and X-rays to two or three clinics before your trip. Compare itemized quotes side by side. Pay in cash (USD or VND) to avoid card surcharges. Consider Korean implant brands, which offer excellent quality at lower prices than European alternatives. And remember, the cheapest option is not always the best value. Focus on the overall package of quality, experience, and aftercare.
