## Services Breakdown

### Dental Fillings
Opal Dental's most popular service among walk-in patients. The clinic uses modern composite resin materials that match natural tooth color. **Fillings start from approximately $6**, making this one of the most affordable options in Vietnam for quality dental restoration work.

### Dental Implants
Implant services start from approximately **$628** using Korean implant systems. The clinic offers a full implant workflow including CT scanning, treatment planning, surgical placement, and prosthetic restoration. European implant brands are available for patients who prefer premium systems.

### Porcelain Crowns and Veneers
The clinic offers metal-ceramic, zirconia, and E.max crown options. Turnaround time is typically 3-5 working days. Veneers are custom-crafted to match existing teeth for a natural appearance.

### Orthodontics
Opal Dental provides traditional braces and clear aligner consultations. The orthodontics department can initiate treatment for patients who plan to stay in Vietnam long enough, or provide assessments and referrals for short-stay tourists.

### Root Canal and General Dentistry
Routine and emergency dental care including root canals, extractions, dental cleanings, and comprehensive check-ups. The clinic's walk-in policy makes it ideal for tourists experiencing unexpected dental issues.
