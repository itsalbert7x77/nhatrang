## Daisy International Dental Clinic Overview

Daisy International Dental Clinic (Nha Khoa Quoc Te Daisy) is a modern dental practice at 83 Yersin Street in Phuong Sai Ward. **Founded in 2022**, the clinic was purpose-built to deliver **European-standard dental care** in Nha Trang, equipped from day one with the latest diagnostic and treatment technology.

With 133 reviews and a 4.8 rating in just a few years of operation, Daisy has quickly established itself among Nha Trang's top dental clinics. The practice distinguishes itself through advanced technology including **3D CBCT scanning, digital X-ray, and intraoral scanning**, combined with a dental team trained at prestigious international institutions.

## The Dental Team

Daisy International Dental assembled its team with a focus on international credentials. The dentists have trained at recognized dental schools across Vietnam and abroad, bringing expertise in implantology, cosmetic dentistry, endodontics, and orthodontics (including Invisalign certification).

The founding vision was to create a practice where the standard of care, equipment, and patient experience match what patients would expect at a top clinic in Seoul, Bangkok, or a European capital, but at Vietnamese pricing. The team's ongoing professional development includes regular training on new technologies and techniques.

English-speaking staff are available for international patient coordination, from initial inquiry through post-treatment follow-up.

## Services Breakdown

### Dental Implants
Daisy's implant service leverages the clinic's 3D scanning capability for computer-guided implant placement. This technology-driven approach improves precision, reduces surgical time, and supports better long-term outcomes. The clinic works with premium implant brands and offers options at multiple price points.

### Invisalign and Orthodontics
As an authorized Invisalign provider, Daisy offers the full range of Invisalign treatments including Invisalign Comprehensive, Invisalign Lite, and Invisalign Go. Intraoral scanning creates a precise digital model, and patients can preview their projected results in 3D before committing to treatment. The clinic can manage international patients' Invisalign treatment remotely after the initial fitting.

### Cosmetic Dentistry
The clinic's cosmetic services include porcelain veneers, smile design, teeth whitening, and composite bonding. Digital smile design software allows patients to see simulated results before treatment. All cosmetic work is performed with attention to natural aesthetics and facial harmony.

### Porcelain Crowns and Veneers
Daisy uses CAD/CAM technology and works with premium materials including zirconia and E.max lithium disilicate. Digital impressions eliminate the discomfort of traditional molds and improve accuracy. Most crown and veneer cases are completed within 3-5 working days.

### Root Canal Treatment
Endodontic procedures are performed using rotary instrumentation and electronic apex locators for precise canal treatment. The clinic's digital X-ray capabilities allow real-time imaging during procedures.

### General Dentistry
Comprehensive examinations, preventive cleanings, fillings, and extractions using the latest materials and techniques. Digital X-rays are included in all comprehensive check-ups.
