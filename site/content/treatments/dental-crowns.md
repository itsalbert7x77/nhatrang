## What Is a Dental Crown?

**A dental crown is a custom-made cap that covers a damaged, decayed, or weakened tooth, restoring its shape, strength, and appearance. In Nha Trang, dental crowns cost just $70-$150 — a saving of up to 90% compared to Australia ($1,000-$2,000) and the USA ($1,000-$2,500). Clinics like Opal Dental (4.8 stars, 234 reviews) and Viet Han Dental (4.8 stars, 119 reviews) use premium materials including zirconia and emax porcelain.**

Crowns are one of the most common dental restorations and are used after root canals, for cracked or broken teeth, to cover severely discoloured teeth, or as the final component of a dental implant. Modern crowns are virtually indistinguishable from natural teeth.

### Types of Dental Crowns Available in Nha Trang

- **Porcelain-fused-to-metal (PFM)** — $70-$90. A metal substructure with porcelain overlay. Durable and affordable, though the metal margin may show over time.
- **Full zirconia** — $100-$130. Extremely strong and natural-looking. Ideal for back teeth that endure heavy chewing forces.
- **Emax (lithium disilicate)** — $120-$150. The most aesthetic option with excellent light transmission. Perfect for front teeth.
- **Full porcelain** — $90-$120. Good aesthetics without metal, suitable for patients with metal sensitivities.

### Recovery Tips

- Avoid chewing on the crowned tooth for the first 24 hours after cementation.
- Some sensitivity to hot and cold is normal for the first week.
- Avoid very hard or sticky foods that could dislodge the new crown.
- Maintain regular brushing and flossing around the crown.
- If the bite feels uneven, return to the clinic for a simple adjustment.
