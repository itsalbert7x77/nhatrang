### Types of Extractions

- **Simple extraction** ($15-$25) — For fully erupted teeth that are visible above the gumline. Quick procedure using local anaesthesia.
- **Surgical extraction** ($30-$50) — For impacted, broken, or teeth with curved roots requiring gum incision and possibly bone removal.
- **Wisdom tooth extraction** ($25-$50) — Can be simple or surgical depending on the position and angulation of the wisdom tooth.

### Recovery Tips

- Bite down firmly on the gauze pad for 30-45 minutes after extraction to control bleeding.
- Do not spit, use a straw, or smoke for 24 hours — this can dislodge the blood clot and cause dry socket.
- Apply ice packs to the cheek (20 minutes on, 20 minutes off) to reduce swelling.
- Eat soft, cool foods for the first 24-48 hours. Avoid hot liquids.
- Gently rinse with warm salt water starting 24 hours after the extraction.
- Take prescribed antibiotics and pain medication as directed.
