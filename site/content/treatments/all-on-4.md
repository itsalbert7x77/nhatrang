## What Are All-on-4 Dental Implants?

**All-on-4 is a revolutionary full-arch tooth replacement technique that uses just four strategically placed implants to support an entire set of fixed teeth. In Nha Trang, All-on-4 costs $3,500-$7,000 per arch — saving up to 80% compared to Australia ($20,000-$35,000) and the USA ($20,000-$30,000). Leading clinics like Viet Han Dental (4.8 stars, 119 reviews), Opal Dental (4.8 stars, 234 reviews), and Dental Clinic Dr. Nguyen (4.8 stars, 151 reviews) have experienced implantologists performing this life-changing procedure.**

All-on-4 is the most cost-effective way to replace a full arch of missing or failing teeth with a fixed, non-removable prosthesis. The angled posterior implants maximise contact with available bone, often eliminating the need for costly bone grafting. Patients receive a temporary set of teeth on the same day as surgery, walking out of the clinic with a complete smile.

### How the Procedure Works

1. **Pre-surgical planning** — CBCT 3D scan and digital planning to determine optimal implant positions.
2. **Extractions** — Any remaining teeth in the arch are removed.
3. **Implant placement** — Four implants are inserted: two straight in the front, two angled in the back.
4. **Immediate temporary prosthesis** — A pre-fabricated temporary bridge is secured to the implants the same day.
5. **Healing period** — 3-6 months of osseointegration while wearing the temporary teeth.
6. **Permanent prosthesis** — A custom zirconia or high-grade acrylic bridge replaces the temporary one.

### Recovery Tips

- Stick exclusively to soft or liquid foods for the first 2 weeks (soups, smoothies, yoghurt, mashed potatoes).
- Gradually introduce semi-soft foods from weeks 2-6, avoiding anything hard or crunchy.
- Take all prescribed antibiotics and anti-inflammatory medications.
- Sleep with your head elevated for the first few nights to reduce swelling.
- Do not smoke — smoking is the number one risk factor for implant failure.
- Apply ice packs to the face (20 minutes on, 20 off) for the first 48 hours.
- Rinse gently with prescribed antimicrobial mouthwash. Do not use vigorous rinsing.
- Attend all follow-up appointments before leaving Nha Trang.
