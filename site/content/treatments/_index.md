## Most Popular Treatments

**Dental implants** are the number one reason international patients visit Nha Trang. A single implant costs $500-$1,400 here versus $3,000-$6,000 in the US — savings of up to 80%. With 20+ clinics competing, patients benefit from some of Vietnam's best implant pricing. Read our [dental implant savings breakdown](/posts/dental-implant-savings-vietnam/) for a detailed cost comparison.

**Veneers and crowns** are the second most popular category. A full set of 8 porcelain veneers runs $1,040-$2,800 in Nha Trang compared to $8,000-$16,000 in Australia. Many patients combine veneers with [teeth whitening](/treatments/teeth-whitening/) for a complete smile makeover.

**Teeth cleaning and check-ups** are popular with expats and long-stay tourists. At just $15-20 for a professional cleaning, routine care is remarkably affordable in Nha Trang.
