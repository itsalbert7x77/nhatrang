## What Is a Root Canal?

**A root canal is an endodontic procedure that removes infected pulp tissue from inside a tooth, saving it from extraction. In Nha Trang, root canals cost $50-$150 — saving up to 90% compared to Australia ($800-$2,000) and the USA ($700-$1,500). Root canal treatment has a reputation for being painful, but modern techniques and anaesthesia make it a routine, comfortable procedure. The real pain comes from the infection itself — a root canal eliminates that pain and saves the natural tooth. Without treatment, an infected tooth must be extracted.

### When You Need a Root Canal

- Severe, persistent toothache that worsens with pressure or heat
- Prolonged sensitivity to hot or cold temperatures
- Darkening or discolouration of the tooth
- Swelling or tenderness in the nearby gums
- A visible abscess (pimple-like bump) on the gum near the tooth

### Recovery Tips

- Take prescribed pain medication as directed. Mild discomfort is normal for 2-3 days.
- Avoid chewing on the treated tooth until the permanent crown is placed.
- Do not eat hard or crunchy foods on that side for the first few days.
- Maintain normal brushing and flossing, being gentle around the treated area.
- Contact the clinic if pain worsens significantly or swelling increases after 48 hours.
