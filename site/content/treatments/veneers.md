**Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin shells bonded to the front of your teeth to create a flawless, natural-looking smile. ### Types of Veneers Available

- **Composite veneers** — $130-$170. Applied directly to the tooth in a single visit. Less durable but more affordable and reversible.
- **Standard porcelain veneers** — $180-$250. Custom-made in a dental lab. Excellent aesthetics and durability.
- **Emax veneers** — $250-$350. The premium option with superior translucency and colour matching. The most natural-looking result.
- **No-prep/minimal-prep veneers** — $200-$300. Ultra-thin veneers requiring little to no enamel removal.

### Recovery Tips

- Avoid very hot or cold foods for the first 48 hours after bonding.
- Do not bite into hard foods (apples, corn on the cob) directly — cut them first.
- Wear a night guard if you grind or clench your teeth.
- Avoid stain-causing foods and drinks (coffee, red wine, turmeric) for the first week.
- Brush gently with a soft-bristled toothbrush and non-abrasive toothpaste.
