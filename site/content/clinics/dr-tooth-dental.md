Dr. Tooth Dental Clinic (Nha Khoa Dr. Tooth) at 111 Thich Quang Duc Street in Phuoc Hai ward is a clean, professionally run dental practice that has earned strong reviews for its **transparent pricing, spotless facilities, and efficient patient management**. ## The Dental Team

Dr. Tooth Dental employs a focused team of dentists with expertise spanning implantology, orthodontics, oral surgery, and cosmetic dentistry. The clinic prioritizes thorough training and continuing education, with staff regularly updating their skills in modern dental techniques.

The dentists are known for taking time to explain procedures clearly, using visual aids and diagrams to ensure patients understand their treatment options. English communication is available for international patients.

## Services Breakdown

### Dental Implants
The clinic offers a comprehensive implant service from initial assessment through to final restoration. CT scanning and digital planning ensure precise implant placement. Both Korean and European implant systems are available, and the clinic handles bone grafting cases that other practices may refer out.

### Orthodontics and Clear Aligners
Dr. Tooth Dental provides traditional braces (metal and ceramic) alongside clear aligner systems. Digital scanning replaces traditional impressions, and treatment simulation software allows patients to see projected outcomes. The clinic can initiate clear aligner treatment for tourists and arrange for ongoing care remotely.

### Oral Surgery
The surgical department handles wisdom tooth extractions (including impacted teeth), surgical root removal, bone grafting, sinus lifts, and minor jaw procedures. Modern imaging and surgical protocols minimize recovery times and complications.

### Cosmetic Dentistry
Services include porcelain veneers, dental bonding, teeth whitening, and smile design consultations. The clinic emphasizes natural-looking results that complement each patient's facial features.

### General Dentistry
Comprehensive check-ups, cleanings, fillings, root canals, and extractions. The clinic uses digital X-rays and modern materials for all restorative work.
