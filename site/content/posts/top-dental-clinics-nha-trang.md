## Why Nha Trang for Dental Work?

Nha Trang has quietly become one of Vietnam's top dental tourism destinations. The coastal city in Khanh Hoa province offers a combination that is hard to beat: qualified dentists trained to international standards, prices 60-80% lower than Western countries, and a stunning beachside setting for recovery. Unlike the hectic pace of Ho Chi Minh City or Hanoi, Nha Trang lets you recover along Tran Phu Beach with fresh seafood and ocean breezes.

## Our Ranking Methodology

We evaluated each clinic based on Google review scores and volume, treatment range, English-language capability, facility standards, pricing transparency, and feedback from international patients. Every clinic on this list has been verified as currently operating and accepting foreign patients.

## 1. Opal Dental Nha Trang

**Rating:** 4.8 / 5 (234 reviews)

Opal Dental is the most-reviewed dental clinic in Nha Trang among international patients and for good reason. The clinic features modern equipment including 3D CBCT scanning, digital smile design technology, and a dedicated implant surgery suite. Their team includes specialists across implantology, cosmetics, and orthodontics.

**Best for:** Dental implants, porcelain veneers, full-mouth restorations
**Sample prices:** Single dental implant (Korean) from $450, porcelain crown from $120, teeth whitening from $80
**Pros:** Largest review base, modern facility, wide treatment range
**Cons:** Can be busy during peak season, book well in advance

## 2. Dr. Nguyen Dental Clinic

**Rating:** 4.8 / 5 (151 reviews)

Dr. Nguyen's clinic has built a strong reputation through consistently excellent patient outcomes and a personal touch. The lead dentist trained internationally and brings a meticulous approach to every procedure. The clinic is known for taking time with patients and explaining every step.

**Best for:** Complex restorative work, crowns and bridges, root canals
**Sample prices:** Porcelain crown from $110, root canal from $60, dental bridge (3 units) from $330
**Pros:** Highly experienced lead dentist, personal attention, thorough consultations
**Cons:** Smaller clinic, limited appointment slots

## 3. Viet Han Dental Clinic

**Rating:** 4.8 / 5 (119 reviews)

Viet Han Dental operates a well-equipped modern clinic with a strong focus on cosmetic dentistry. They use CAD/CAM technology for same-day crowns and have invested heavily in their laboratory facilities.

**Best for:** Cosmetic dentistry, same-day crowns, veneers
**Sample prices:** Zirconia crown from $150, composite veneer from $80, dental implant (Korean) from $480
**Pros:** CAD/CAM same-day restorations, strong cosmetic focus, good English
**Cons:** Slightly higher prices than some competitors

## 4. Daisy International Dental Clinic

## How to Choose the Right Clinic

**If you want the most-reviewed option:** Opal Dental has the largest base of verified international patient reviews.

**If you want personal attention:** Dr. Nguyen's smaller clinic means more one-on-one time with the lead dentist.

**If you want same-day crowns:** Viet Han Dental's CAD/CAM technology can produce crowns in a single visit.

**If this is your first dental tourism trip:** Daisy International's concierge approach removes the guesswork.

**If you are on a tight budget:** Nhan Tin offers the lowest prices in Nha Trang without compromising on essential quality.

## Our Advice

Request a free consultation from two or three clinics before committing. Send your dental X-rays by email and compare the treatment plans and quotes you receive. Every clinic on this list is capable of delivering quality work, so your decision should come down to the specific treatment you need, the dentist's experience with that procedure, and how comfortable you feel with their communication.
