## Viet Han Dental Clinic Overview

Viet Han Dental Clinic (Nha Khoa Viet Han) occupies a prominent triple-shophouse location at 56-58-60 Quang Trung Street in the heart of Nha Trang. Ranked as the **#1 dental clinic in Nha Trang**, the practice has built its reputation on a combination of specialist expertise, modern technology, and pricing that remains highly competitive even by Vietnamese standards.

The clinic spans multiple floors with dedicated departments for implantology, orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and general care. With over 10 specialist dentists holding international certifications, patients rarely need to be referred elsewhere regardless of the complexity of their case.

## The Dental Team

Viet Han Dental employs more than 10 specialist dentists, each certified in their respective fields through Vietnamese and international training programs. The team includes dedicated implantologists, orthodontists, prosthodontists, and endodontists. Several dentists have trained in South Korea and Japan, bringing back techniques and standards from those advanced dental markets.

All dentists at Viet Han participate in continuing education programs and regularly attend international dental conferences to stay current with the latest techniques and materials.

## Services Breakdown

### Dental Implants and All-on-4
Viet Han Dental is one of the few clinics in Nha Trang offering a full range of implant solutions including single implants, implant bridges, and full-arch All-on-4 and All-on-6 restorations. The clinic works with multiple implant brands including Korean (Osstem, Dentium) and European (Straumann, Nobel Biocare) systems to suit different budgets.

### Orthodontics
The orthodontics department handles traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligner systems. Digital scanning eliminates the need for uncomfortable impressions, and treatment planning is done using 3D simulation software.

### Porcelain Crowns and Veneers
The clinic offers a range of crown materials from metal-ceramic to full zirconia and E.max porcelain. Veneers are custom-designed using digital smile design technology. Most crown and veneer cases can be completed within 3-5 working days.

### General and Preventive Dentistry
Routine services include dental cleanings, fillings, root canal therapy, extractions, and comprehensive check-ups. The clinic uses digital X-rays for lower radiation exposure and instant results.
