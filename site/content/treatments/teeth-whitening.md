### How the Procedure Works

1. **Preparation** — Your dentist examines your teeth and may perform a cleaning for optimal results.
2. **Gum protection** — A barrier gel or rubber dam is applied to shield the gums from the bleaching agent.
3. **Gel application** — Professional-strength hydrogen peroxide gel is applied to the teeth.
4. **Light activation** — An LED or laser light accelerates the whitening process.
5. **Multiple cycles** — The gel is applied 2-3 times in 15-20 minute intervals.
6. **Final rinse** — The gel is removed and a desensitising agent may be applied.

### Recovery Tips

- Avoid coffee, tea, red wine, and dark-coloured foods for 48 hours after whitening.
- Do not smoke for at least 48 hours post-treatment.
- Use a sensitivity toothpaste if you experience any discomfort.
- Drink staining beverages through a straw to minimise contact with teeth.
- Maintain results with good brushing habits and regular dental cleanings.
